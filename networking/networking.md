## Comands/Scripts

- List all listening PORTS with netstat: `netstat -nap tcp | grep -i "listen"`
- SSH tunnel: `ssh -L local_port:remote_address:remote_port username@ssh_server.com`
- Port scan with nmap: `namp -Pn <ip>`
